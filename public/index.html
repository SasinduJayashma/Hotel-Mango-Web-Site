Discover Hotel Mango

Get To Know What We're All About

We here at Hotel Mango truly understand that the modern day traveller uses hotels as a launch pad to exploring a destination and what it has to offer. Leveraging this insight, each hotel captures what defines the destination’s spirit to ensure a truly authentic experience, from pristine beaches to the vast culture the Island has to offer. Experiences, design and culinary offerings are influenced by the destination’s personality translating to a holistic guest experience differing from property to property.

Why Book With Us

When you arrive for business, work fast with an array of modern facilities to suit your busy lifestyle. And when you stay for leisure, slow down with a spa, infinity pool and one of Colombo’s highest rooftop bars. Our selection of contemporary facilities is inspired by our fast-developing island capital, and has been designed to serve you with the finest of Sri Lankan hospitality.
